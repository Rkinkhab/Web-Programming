My Education

Stevens Institute of Technology

Stevens institute of technology is a private university located in Hoboken, New Jersey. It was founded in 1870.

Degree: Masters In Computer Science

Favourite Class: My favourite class from Stevens was CS561 Database Management Systems. This class was taught by Prof Samuel Kim. Databases being one of my favourite areas of interest I enjoyed going to this class. Also, Prof Kim was an excellent professor.

Memorable memory: Being an international student from India studying in Stevens was indeed a new experience for me. The most memorable memory about Stevens was the Diwali celebration organised by IGSA.

C.K Pithawalla college of Engineering and Technology

C.K.P.C.E.T is an undergraduate engineering college located in Gujarat,India. It is under University of Gujarat Technological university.

Degree: Bachelor of Engineering - Computer Engineering

Favourite Class: My favourite class was Distributed Systems class.

Memorable Memory: Our college used to organise a cultural festival every year in the month of February called 'Surge'. This festival used to have a number of events related to dance, music, singing, sports and also a DJ night. I used to enjoy going to this festival with my friends. This is one my most memorable memory from my undergraduate college.

M.T Jariwala High School

M.T jariwala High school is a secondary school located in Gujarat, India. It is named after great thinker 'Swami Vivekanand'.

Degree: Secondary School Certificate

Favourite Class: My favourite class from my high school was English which was taught by Mr Atul Tambakuwala.

Memorable Memory: The most memorable memory I have about by school is our school's Annual Day celebration. Our class had performed a group dance on Indian movie songs. We had also won the best performance award.
